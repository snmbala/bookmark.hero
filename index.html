Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

Bookmark Hero is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your browsing data should remain private and under your complete control.

Zero Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data, including:

  • Your bookmarks or browsing history
  • Website content or URLs you visit
  • Personal information or identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Search queries or preferences

Local Processing

All bookmark processing happens locally on your device. Your data never leaves your browser and is never sent to our servers or any third parties. Everything stays on your computer.

Required Permissions

Bookmark Hero requires access to your bookmarks to provide its functionality. This permission is used solely to read and display your bookmarks within the extension interface. We cannot and do not access any other browser data.

Security

Since all data processing occurs locally, your bookmark data never travels across the internet, making it inherently secure from external threats.
