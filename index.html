Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Visual Thumbnails

Auto-captured screenshots for every bookmark. See what a page looks like before you click.

Instant Search

Search across titles, URLs, and domains in real time. Find any bookmark in under a second.

Folder Filtering

Filter by one or multiple folders at once. Active filters are shown as removable tags above the grid.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Navigate cards with arrow keys, open, edit, recapture, delete — entirely hands-free. Press ? to see all shortcuts.

Privacy First

Everything runs locally on your device. No data is ever collected, stored remotely, or shared with anyone.

Smart Storage

Thumbnails compress to 40 KB automatically. Delete a bookmark and its preview is cleaned up instantly.

How it works

Up and running in seconds

1

Install the extension

Click "Add to Chrome" from the Web Store. No account or sign-up needed.

2

Capture thumbnails

Use "Capture All Missing" in Settings to generate screenshots for all your existing bookmarks.

3

Browse your gallery

Search, filter, and navigate your bookmarks visually. New bookmarks are captured automatically.
